Introduction

Welcome to Daily JP Step ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do NOT collect any personal information.

Daily JP Step is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. All your data, including:

  • Learning progress
  • Favorite words
  • App preferences
  • Quiz scores

...is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any external servers.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob

We display advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may collect certain information for ad personalization. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by the operating system. We do not have access to this data, and it is not backed up to any cloud service by our app.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences interested in learning Japanese.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
