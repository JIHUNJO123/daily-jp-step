Introduction

HoonieLab ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Daily JP Step mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

Information Collection

Daily JP Step is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Learning Progress: Your study progress, favorites, and quiz scores are stored locally on your device only.
  • No Account Required: We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the app.
  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect names, email addresses, or other personally identifiable information.

Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob: We display advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may collect device information and advertising identifiers to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: When you make in-app purchases, these platforms process the transaction. We do not have access to your payment information.

Data Storage

All your learning data (1200+ phrases progress, favorites, quiz scores) is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to our servers.

Children Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app can be used without providing any personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
